Elon Musk

CEO of Tesla and SpaceX

Elon Musk, CEO of Tesla

Here's a time line of Elon's life:

  • 1971 - Born in Pretoria, South Africa.
  • 1989 - He moved to Canada
  • 1990 - Musk entered Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario
  • 1992 - He transferred to the University of Pennsylvania
  • 1994 - Musk held two internships in Silicon Valley during the summer: at an energy storage start-up called Pinnacle Research Institute and at the Palo Alto-based start-up Rocket Science Games
  • 1997 - He graduated with a Bachelor of Science (BS) degree in economics from the Wharton School and a Bachelor of Arts (BA) degree in physics.
  • 1995 - Musk, his brother Kimbal, and Greg Kouri founded web software company Zip2
  • 1999 - Compaq aquired zip2 for $307 million in cash. Musk received $22 million for his 7 percent share from the sale.
  • 2002 - Ebay bought the company PayPal with 1.5 billion dollars and Musk received $100 million. With this money he founded SpaceX.
  • 2004 - Musk became the CEO of Tesla.
